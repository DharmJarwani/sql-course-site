MIN and MAX – Get the Smallest & Largest Values

Quickly find the minimum or maximum value from a column of data.

When do we use MIN and MAX?

Use these functions to find extreme values in numeric or date columns — great for analysis and reporting.

  • Earliest and latest orders or hires
  • Minimum or maximum price, discount, or freight
  • Lowest or highest quantity sold

Basic Syntax

SELECT 
   MIN(column_name) AS Smallest,
   MAX(column_name) AS Largest
FROM table_name;

Example 1 – Earliest and Latest Orders

SELECT 
   MIN(OrderDate) AS EarliestOrder,
   MAX(OrderDate) AS LatestOrder
FROM Orders;
