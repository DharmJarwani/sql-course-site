DATES – Handling Dates in SQL

Use SQL date functions to filter, format, and calculate with date and time values.

Why Dates Matter?

Dates are essential for filtering, reporting, and tracking activities. SQL offers powerful date functions to work with date columns in your tables.

  • Filter records by date ranges
  • Extract parts like year, month, or day
  • Calculate age, duration, or trends

1 – Filter by Specific Date

SELECT * 
FROM Orders
WHERE OrderDate = '1997-01-01';

2 – Orders Placed in a Specific Year

SELECT * 
FROM Orders
WHERE YEAR(OrderDate) = 1997;

3 – Extracting Month and Day

SELECT 
  OrderID, 
  MONTH(OrderDate) AS OrderMonth,
  DAY(OrderDate) AS OrderDay
FROM Orders;

4 – Orders Between Two Dates

SELECT * 
FROM Orders
WHERE OrderDate 
  BETWEEN '1997-01-01' AND '1997-12-31';
