BETWEEN – Range Filtering

Filter values that fall within a specific range — numeric, date, or text.

When do we use BETWEEN?

The BETWEEN operator lets you filter rows where a value is **within a defined range**, inclusive of the boundary values. It works with numbers, dates, and even alphabetical ranges.

  • Find orders placed between two dates
  • List products with prices in a target range
  • Get customers with IDs between a lower and upper bound

BETWEEN Syntax

SELECT * 
FROM Orders
WHERE OrderDate BETWEEN '1997-01-01' AND '1997-01-31';

Example 1 – Orders in January 1997

SELECT OrderID, CustomerID, OrderDate
FROM Orders
WHERE OrderDate BETWEEN '1997-01-01' AND '1997-01-31';
