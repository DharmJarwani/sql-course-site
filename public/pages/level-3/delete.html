DELETE – Remove Data

Permanently remove one or more rows from a table.

When do we use DELETE?

The DELETE statement is used to remove unwanted or outdated records from a table. Always use a WHERE clause unless you intend to clear all rows!

  • Deleting inactive users or old orders
  • Removing test or sample data
  • Data cleanup during migration

Syntax

DELETE FROM table_name
WHERE condition;

Example – Remove Cancelled Orders

DELETE FROM Orders
WHERE Status = 'Cancelled';
